Getting Here
Public Transport

Bus routes 15, 27, and 42 stop directly at University Campus. The nearest train station is Academic Central (10-minute walk).

Parking

Free parking available in Visitor Car Park A. Disabled parking spaces available near the main entrance.

Cycling

Secure bike parking available. Campus is part of the National Cycle Network Route 21.
